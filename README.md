# Kibo

AI Community Manager for Streamers

## What is Kibo?

Kibo is an AI-powered community manager designed for creators and streamers.

Instead of hiring multiple moderators or manually answering repetitive questions, creators can deploy Kibo to manage their community across live streams.

Kibo understands the creator's content, history, FAQs, community guidelines, and brand voice to answer questions, moderate discussions, and keep the community engaged.

---

## Mission

Help creators focus on creating.

Let Kibo handle the community.

---

## Problem

Streamers spend a significant amount of time:

- Answering repetitive questions
- Managing chat
- Moderating spam
- Sharing links repeatedly
- Handling community FAQs

Small creators often have no moderators.

Large creators require multiple moderators.

Both face the same challenge:

Too much chat. Not enough attention.

---

## Solution

Kibo acts as an AI Community Manager.

It joins live chats and helps answer questions on behalf of creators.

Examples:

- What microphone do you use?
- What game is this?
- When is the next stream?
- Where is the Discord server?
- What setup do you use?

Kibo answers instantly.
